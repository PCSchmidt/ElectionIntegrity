APPROVED FOR PUBLIC RELEASE BY COUNSEL TO THE PRESIDENT WARRINGTON on 10 July 2026 UNCLASSIFIED//FOUO (U) Intelligence Program Policy Guide ~ 9.6.3. (U) Public Corruption Information Report Documentation (U//FOUO) Disseminated PCIRs must be stored in Sentinel. PCIRs must be serialized to the INTELPRODS subfiles of the investigative Assessments or case files from which their information was derived. All PCIRs must be serialized to file 804B-HQ-C3043777 in Sentinel. 9.6.4. (U) BLUEGEM Report Documentation (U) BLG reports must be documented according to the procedures outlined in subsection 4.6. of the Bureau Control System BLUEGEM Policy Guide (1012PG) [links to a SECRET//NOFORN document]. 9.7.1.1. (U) Limited Distribution of Intelligence Information Reports (U//FOUO) A limited-distribution ITR must be held to the same dissemination thresholds as a standard-dissemination IIR. Limited-distribution IIRs may only be used for circumstances in which the reported intelligence is exceptionally sensitive in nature. When drafting a limited-distribution IIR, the distribution list may not be so limited that the IIR is released to a single agency or solely within the FBI. (U//FOUO) For more information and guidance on limited-distribution IIRs, see the Raw Intelligence Reports Manual. (U//FOUO) ODNI guidelines do not allow for the modification of a subject line or the administrative page. If either change is necessary, the entire IIR must be recalled and reissued with a new serial number. Report authors must not cite the original report or the administrative recall in the reissued report. (U//FOUO) For more guidance on recalling or revising IIRs, see the Raw Intelligence Reports Manual. 9.7.2. (U) Dissemination, Revision, and Recall Procedures for Situational Information Reports and Public Corruption Information Reports (U//FOUO) FBIHQ divisions/FOs authoring SIRs must ensure that the approved SIRs are disseminated to the appropriate state, local, and tribal LE partners. Revision and recall procedures for SIRs and PCIRs must follow the same processes mandated for IIRs in subsection 9.7.1. of this PG. 48 UNCLASSIFIED//FOUO
